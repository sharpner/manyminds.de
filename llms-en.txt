## What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are autonomous software systems that complete tasks independently. Unlike simple chatbots, they can:
- Execute complex workflows
- Make decisions
- Interact with various systems
- Learn from experience
- Understand natural language

## Common Questions

**Which companies are AI agents suitable for?**
AI agents are suitable for companies of all sizes with repetitive processes that require human judgment. Especially effective in customer service, data processing, research, and process control.

**How do AI agents differ from traditional automation?**
Traditional automation follows rigid rules. AI agents can handle uncertainty, learn, and flexibly respond to new situations.
